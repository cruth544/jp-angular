PLEASE NOTE: TRANSPORTATION - If you are flying, please note that flights are occasionally canceled traveling to and from Mammoth due to smoke from forest fires and high winds in the Sierras. If you plan on flying, please be sure to arrive a few days in advance. ELEVATION - Please note that the wedding venue is located at 9,600ft elevation. If you are susceptible to elevation sickness, please arrive several days earlier in order to acclimate to the elevation. The center of town is approximately 7,600ft elevation. Also, please note that the effects of alcohol are accelerated at higher altitudes. Party safe and responsibly - our event will be heavily photographed :) WEATHER - Temperatures in Mammoth during August generally vary between an average high during the day of 80 degrees and a low of 50 degrees. Please dress accordingly.
